About

Sai Pallavi was born on 9 May 1992 in a Badaga Hindu family in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Her hometown is Kotagiri in the Nilgiris district, Tamil Nadu. Born to Senthamarai Kannan and Radha, she has a younger sister, Pooja Kannan, who has also worked as an actress.

Back Ground

Sai Pallavi appeared in uncredited roles as a child actress in Kasthuri Maan (2005) and Dhaam Dhoom (2008). She had also done a Tamil Short Film named 'Kaatchi Pizhai' before her debut in the movies. Pallavi debuted as a main Female lead in the 2015 movie Premam alongside Nivin Pauly.
